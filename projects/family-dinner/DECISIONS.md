# Family Dinner - Decisions

## Decision Criteria

### Valid Dinner

A dinner is VALID when:
1. Has at least one main dish
2. Main dish is child-acceptable
3. Menu is simple enough for household execution
4. At least one person confirmed acceptance

A dinner is NOT VALID if:
- No child-acceptable dish exists

### Dish Status Upgrade

A one-time observation becomes a durable rule when:
1. Same feedback stated explicitly 2+ times
2. Explicit statement from person confirming preference
3. Other affected person has opportunity to object

### Vague Feedback

Do NOT upgrade to rule when:
- "Normal", "done", "looks fine" (not explicit approval)
- Single occurrence without follow-up
- Contradicted by subsequent behavior
